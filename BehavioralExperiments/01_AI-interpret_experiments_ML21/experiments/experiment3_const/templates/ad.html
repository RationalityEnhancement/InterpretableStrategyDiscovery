Call for participants

The Rationality Enhancement Group at the Max-Planck-Institute for Intelligent Systems Tuebingen (Germany) is looking for online participants for an experiment on decision making.

You will have the opportunity to earn a bonus of up to $6 in addition to the base payment of $3. A typical payment is $6.5. The experiment will take roughly 50 minutes to complete.

The requirements for participation are:

  • You are at least 18 years old,
  • You have normal vision or wear corrective lenses, and
  • You are a fluent English speaker.
  • You have not previously participated in one of our experiments.

Note: the experiment will only work in recent browsers, and may have difficulty loading on slower connections.

This task can only be completed once. If you have already started or completed this task the system will not allow you to run again. If you have previously accepted a HIT with the same title as this one, please return this HIT.
